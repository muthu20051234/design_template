Privacy Policy Effective 06 September 2025

Mokshambani Tech Services Private Limited (“we”, “us”, “our”) operates the mobile application Ambani Dail. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our app and related services.

1) Information We Collect

  • Account Information: name, email, phone number, profile photo/URL, user ID (e.g., Google UID) when you sign in with Google or OTP.

  • Bookings & Posts: services you browse, post, request, or book, ratings/reviews, and communication metadata.
  • Device & App Data: device model, OS version, app version, language, approximate IP-derived location, crash logs.
  • Location Data (with your permission): precise or approximate location for features like showing nearby services, maps/navigation, or provider tracking.

2) How We Collect Information

  • Directly from you when you create/update an account, post a need, or make a booking.

  • To operate and improve the app, display listings, process bookings, and show relevant results near you.

  • To send service notifications, OTPs, and important updates (e.g., booking status, payment confirmation).
  • To provide customer support and respond to your requests.
  • To comply with law and enforce our Terms.

4) Legal Bases (where applicable)

We process your information based on your consent, our legitimate interests in providing and improving the service, and to perform a contract with you (e.g., processing a booking or payment), and/or compliance with legal obligations.
